frequently asked queries

Why should I invest in a landing page?

Your company is like a person, it needs your own identity that makes you unique and people need to know in a digital way that your business exists. In addition, it is a great way to gain new customers, as it adds value to your brand, facilitates communication and gains public and visibility.

How do I hire your services?

The first step is for you to contact me and explain what you need for your business and we will arrange an online meeting. After I have important information that will help me not only to carry out your project, but also to reach at an ideal budget for you.

How to make the payment?

Payment can be made via bank transfer. It is 50% in advance and the rest in the presentation of the project.

What is the average time for project delivery?

The project is presented only when I am sure it is finished and represents all the values that the brand needs to convey. The deadline is given along with the budget, and may vary according to its complexity.
